The Reversed Letter

In the hallway of 32 Windsor Gardens, Mrs. Bird discovers a letter tucked behind the umbrella stand. The envelope is marked in elegant script:

"WHEN ALL SEEMS BACKWARDS,
LOOK TO THE STARS"

Paddington opens it with trembling paws. Inside, Mr. Gruber has left a note:

"My dear Paddington, this is what scholars call 'mirror writing' — each letter swapped for its opposite in the alphabet. We call it Atbash. I've provided you the key below."

The Atbash Key:

A↔Z, B↔Y, C↔X, D↔W, E↔V, F↔U, G↔T, H↔S, I↔R, J↔Q,
K↔P, L↔O, M↔N...

The encrypted message reads:

HGZI FIHZ NRMLI

"Once decoded," Paddington reads on, "the words will point you toward something to count. Bears have excellent counting skills, you know."

Your answer is merely a number.
